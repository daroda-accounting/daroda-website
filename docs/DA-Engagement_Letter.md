# Daroda Accounting Website Migration Engagement Letter

## Engagement Overview

This engagement outlines the planned migration and modernization of the Daroda Accounting website from the Wix platform to a more scalable, maintainable, and performance‑oriented web architecture.

The project is being completed as part of the Developer’s professional internship requirement and is designed to deliver immediate operational continuity while establishing strong technical foundations for future business growth, marketing capability, and potential gated educational offerings.

The intent of this document is to create clarity around scope, priorities, responsibilities, timeline expectations, and long‑term sustainability principles.

---

## Business Objectives & Project Intent

The primary goals of this engagement are:

- Preserve existing booking and lead generation functionality
- Improve technical performance, reliability, and scalability
- Establish proper SEO structure to support future traffic growth
- Reduce long‑term maintenance complexity and vendor dependency
- Prepare architectural foundations for future features such as gated course content, improved lead funnels, or marketing automation

The existing Wix website will remain active during development to ensure uninterrupted business operations.

---

## Scope of Services

### Included Services (MVP Phase)

**Website Migration & Rebuild**
- Rebuild the existing Wix website on a modern hosting platform (e.g., React + static hosting or equivalent)
- Maintain current branding, messaging, and overall page structure unless otherwise agreed
- Improve responsiveness, performance, and technical structure

**Content Migration**
- Transfer written content, imagery, downloadable resources, and primary calls‑to‑action
- Perform minor restructuring for clarity or usability where beneficial

**Booking Funnel Preservation**
- Maintain Calendly scheduling integrations
- Ensure all booking flows remain functional

**Lead Capture Improvements**
- Rebuild inquiry and contact mechanisms
- Implement reliable submission handling and notification workflows
- Prepare structure for future automation enhancements

**Technical SEO Foundations**
- Proper routing structure
- Metadata and semantic markup
- Sitemap creation and indexing readiness
- Performance optimization

**Analytics Setup**
- Configure baseline analytics tooling (e.g., Google Analytics)
- Enable traffic measurement capability

**Deployment & Hosting Setup**
- Deploy site to modern hosting platform
- Assist with DNS configuration and go‑live coordination

### Post‑MVP Enhancements (If Time Allows Within Internship Hours)

- Administrative content update capability planning or prototype
- Lead funnel refinement
- Additional SEO improvements
- Preparation for gated content or course delivery capability

### Excluded Services

Unless added via written change request:

- Paid marketing campaign execution
- Full learning management system implementation
- CRM deployment
- Newsletter marketing system implementation
- Ongoing SEO content production
- Long‑term maintenance agreements

### Change Requests

New features or substantial revisions may require revised timeline, additional hours, or separate agreement.

---

## Timeline & Phased Implementation

The project is estimated at approximately **220 total implementation hours** and will follow a phased delivery model prioritizing rapid MVP completion.

### Phase 1 — Discovery & Technical Planning (~20 hours)

- Review Wix structure and integrations
- Confirm hosting and deployment approach
- Perform SEO baseline and URL mapping
- Identify risks and migration checklist

**Outcome:** Approved migration plan.

### Phase 2 — Core Website Migration (MVP) (~100 hours)

- Rebuild layout and page structure
- Migrate content and resources
- Recreate forms and booking integrations
- Apply performance and SEO improvements
- Configure analytics and deployment pipeline

**Outcome:** Modern site ready for staging review.

### Phase 3 — Testing & Launch (~25 hours)

- Functional validation
- Performance verification
- DNS cutover and launch monitoring

**Outcome:** Live modern website with minimal disruption.

### Phase 4 — Growth Readiness Enhancements (~Remaining Hours)

- Admin capability planning
- Content structure refinement
- Lead funnel improvements
- Architectural preparation for future course capability

**Outcome:** Platform positioned for scalable growth.

### Timeline Flexibility

Timeline may shift due to feedback cycles, content changes, technical discoveries, or business priority adjustments.

---

## Deliverables & Acceptance Criteria

The engagement will be considered successfully completed when:

- The new website is deployed on the Client’s domain
- Core pages and booking flows function reliably
- Analytics tooling is configured
- The Client has access to hosting, repository, and relevant accounts
- No critical technical defects remain

Basic documentation will be provided to support operational continuity.

## Responsibilities & Collaboration Model

### Developer Responsibilities

- Implement website architecture and migration
- Configure hosting and deployment workflows
- Improve performance and technical SEO readiness
- Preserve booking and lead capture functionality
- Provide best‑practice guidance for future scalability
- Structure the project for **future maintenance handoff readiness**

### Client Responsibilities

- Maintain ownership of all business accounts and billing relationships
- Provide timely access, content clarification, and feedback
- Make final decisions regarding priorities and design direction
- Continue operational lead handling during development

### Collaboration Expectations

The engagement will be conducted collaboratively. Scope adjustments may occur based on remaining project hours and business impact.

---

## Sustainability & Maintainability Principles

This project prioritizes long‑term operational simplicity and flexibility.

Design decisions will favor:

- Widely supported technologies
- Low recurring infrastructure cost
- Clear deployment workflows
- Reduced reliance on fragile integrations

The Developer will make reasonable efforts to support future maintainer transition through:

- Clean project organization
- Environment guidance
- Deployment documentation
- Basic knowledge transfer

The Client retains full ownership of domain, hosting, repository, analytics tools, and third‑party services.

---

## Business Outcome Disclaimer

The Developer provides technical implementation and strategic guidance intended to improve scalability, performance, and growth readiness.

However, the Developer **does not guarantee increases in traffic, search rankings, lead volume, or revenue**, as these depend on external business, market, and marketing factors.
