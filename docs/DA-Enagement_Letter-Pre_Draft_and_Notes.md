



## Scope of Services

The Developer will provide website migration and foundational improvement services for Daroda Accounting, LLC as outlined below.

### Included Services (MVP Phase)

1. **Website Migration & Rebuild**

   * Rebuild the existing Wix website on a modern web platform (e.g., React + static hosting or equivalent).
   * Maintain current branding, messaging, and core page structure unless otherwise agreed.
   * Improve performance, mobile responsiveness, and technical structure.

2. **Content Migration**

   * Transfer existing written content, imagery, downloadable assets, and primary calls-to-action.
   * Minor content restructuring for clarity, layout, or usability as needed.

3. **Booking Funnel Preservation**

   * Maintain existing Calendly booking flow and integration.
   * Ensure all booking links and embedded scheduling functionality continue to operate.

4. **Lead Capture Improvements**

   * Rebuild existing inquiry and download forms.
   * Implement reliable submission handling (email notifications, storage, or equivalent).
   * Prepare structure for future automation enhancements.

5. **Search Engine Optimization Foundations**

   * Implement technical SEO fundamentals, including:

     * Proper page routing and structure
     * Metadata and semantic markup
     * Sitemap and indexing readiness
     * Performance optimization

6. **Analytics & Tracking Setup**

   * Configure basic website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics or equivalent).
   * Enable traffic measurement and future marketing insight.

7. **Deployment & Hosting Setup**

   * Deploy website to a modern hosting platform (e.g., Netlify or equivalent).
   * Assist with DNS configuration and go-live coordination.

## Sustainability & Maintainability Principles

This engagement prioritizes long-term operational stability, low maintenance overhead, and future flexibility for Daroda Accounting.

### Low Maintenance Design Philosophy

The website will be designed and implemented with the goal of minimizing ongoing technical complexity and recurring operational burden. Where possible, the Developer will favor:

* Stable, widely adopted technologies
* Simple deployment workflows
* Low-recurring-cost infrastructure
* Reduced reliance on fragile or highly customized integrations

This approach is intended to support business continuity and reduce the risk of disruption as the business grows.

### Future Maintainer Handoff Readiness

Although the Client will retain full ownership of the source code repository and all infrastructure accounts, the Developer will assist in structuring the project to allow for a smooth transition to any future maintainer.

This includes reasonable efforts to provide:

* Clear project structure and organization
* Environment configuration guidance
* Deployment workflow documentation
* Basic operational knowledge transfer

The goal is to ensure that the website can be responsibly maintained or extended by another qualified professional if needed.

### Ownership & Independence

The Client retains full control over domain, hosting, analytics, scheduling tools, and related third-party services. The Developer’s role is to support proper configuration and implementation rather than create dependency on any single vendor or individual.

### Compensation & Project Value

This engagement is being undertaken primarily as a professional development and internship-equivalent project for the Developer.

As such, traditional market consulting rates are not being applied.

The Developer estimates that comparable professional services for a project of this scope — including website migration, architectural planning, infrastructure setup, and scalability preparation — would typically represent a significantly higher one-time implementation cost and/or recurring consulting fees.

For this engagement, compensation will be structured as follows:

* The Client will remain responsible for all third-party infrastructure and service costs (domain registration, hosting services, SaaS tools, email services, etc.)
* The Developer may charge a **minimal agreed-upon maintenance fee post-migration**, to be determined separately based on actual support needs and system complexity
* No guaranteed ongoing monthly consulting agreement is implied unless mutually agreed upon in writing at a later date

This structure allows the Client to receive substantial technical value while enabling the Developer to gain practical implementation experience, portfolio material, and professional reference.

Both parties acknowledge that the engagement is intended to create long-term business value through improved scalability, maintainability, and future growth readiness.
